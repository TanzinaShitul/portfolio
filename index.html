Tiki online ticketing system

The Tiki Online Ticketing System is a Laravel-based web application that automates bus ticket bookings for a round-trip route between Dhaka and Cox's Bazar, with stops at Comilla and Chittagong. It enables users to easily browse available trips and book tickets.

storekeeper

The Laravel-based storekeeper management system helps store owners efficiently manage products, sales, and prices. It allows adding products with quantities, updating stock after sales, and collaborating with a syndicate consultant to adjust prices. The dashboard displays sales figures for today, yesterday, this month, and last month, while a transaction history page shows all sales records.
